Getting Started

Ready. Set. Trot.

Three simple steps to get your pup into the pack.

1

Ready — Meet & Greet

Contact us to schedule a Meet & Greet. Someone from our team will meet with you to discuss what you're looking for and ensure a compatible fit for your pup and our pack.

2

Set — Create Your Profile

Set up your Time To Pet account and complete your profile. DHPP and Rabies vaccinations required. All pups must be microchipped with an ID tag prior to first service.

3

Trot! — Welcome to the Pack

A trained professional is paired with your pup. Invoices sent semi-monthly — credit card, ApplePay, GooglePay, e-transfer, personal check, or auto-charge all accepted.

Our Story

Dogs Remind Us How to Be Present

At DogTrot, we believe dogs remind us how to slow down. They take in the world with every sniff, every squint toward the sun, every joyful hop forward. Their excitement turns a simple walk into something meaningful — for them and for us.

Our pack hikes are more than exercise. They support your dog's physical health, stimulate their mind, and help them connect and socialize in a safe, structured way.

Because movement, curiosity, and companionship aren't just good for dogs — they're good for all of us. Trot with us.
